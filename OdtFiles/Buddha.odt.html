In my Buddhist practice every morning, I will go a week not doing this because I’m lazy but it only takes a couple of minutes. I take ‘Refuge’ like the old sailing ships taking refuge in a port so not to be destroyed by the storm. This reminds me, and it must be done often, that I am NOT the center of the known Universe many times I think I am. So I light a small tea candle at the modest shrine, 2 pictures, and a stick of incense. Here is me and the / Christian version.


With my hands together as in prayer:


Over my head

I take refuge in my guru / priest


At my forehead

I take refuge in The Buddha (just a man) / Jesus Christ son of God (I went to Sunday School)


At my mouth

I take refuge in The Dharma / Holy Bible


At my heart

I take refuge in The Sangha / Monks Nuns and Church Ladies


Lowering myself to my knees and touching my forehead to the floor, this is repeated 3 times.

I must do this because I am not always very humble, it drives home that I should be.
